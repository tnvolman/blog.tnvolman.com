My Story

"I grew up in a non-Christian home in a trailer court in Knoxville, Tennessee. At 11 years old, a deacon from West Park Baptist Church began visiting me every Saturday — bringing candy, bringing love, bringing Jesus. A church bus came every Sunday. Some Sundays I hid and didn't go. But he kept showing up. Through his faithful, consistent love, I gave my life to Jesus. It changed the trajectory of my life forever."

"On April 25, 2026, at the $TRUMP Conference and Gala at Mar-a-Lago, God put something on my heart I have not been able to shake. Everything in my life — every season, every detour, every skill learned — led to that moment. He gave me a vision for technology that actually serves the Church the way He intended. Not a chatbot with a Bible verse. Something genuinely grounded in Scripture. I came home and started building. I haven't stopped.

I am building The Forge — because a deacon showed up for me. Now I want to show up for every pastor who carries weight alone at 2am with no one to call."
